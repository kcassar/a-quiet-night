QUESTIONS FOR YOUR SLEEP CLINIC
===============================

Educational only — adapt these to your situation.

About your therapy
[ ] What pressure (or pressure range) am I currently set to?
[ ] What's my recent average AHI on therapy?
[ ] Is my leak data within an acceptable range?
[ ] How many central vs obstructive events am I having?
[ ] Is my mode (CPAP / APAP / BiPAP) still the right one for me?

About comfort
[ ] What can we try if pressure feels too high?
[ ] What can we try for dry mouth or blocked nose?
[ ] Could I try a different mask style?
[ ] Could heated tubing or a different humidity setting help me?

About results
[ ] Has my therapy improved measurably since I started?
[ ] If I'm still tired, what could be causing it?
[ ] Should I have a follow-up sleep study?

About lifestyle
[ ] Any positional advice for my type of apnoea?
[ ] Any guidance on alcohol or sedating medications?
[ ] If weight loss is relevant, who can help me plan it safely?

About logistics
[ ] How often should I replace mask, hose, filters, water chamber?
[ ] Do I qualify for funded replacements?
[ ] What's the plan if my equipment fails when I'm away from home?

This checklist is informational only and does not replace clinical advice.
